Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class MODEL::ResistiveJunction< T >

ResistiveJunction.

Models a junction with arbitrary resistive inlets and outlets. Across all inlets and outlets of the junction, mass is conserved.

Governing equations

Local contributions

Mass conservation
